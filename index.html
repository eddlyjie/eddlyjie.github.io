I am an incoming PhD student in Computer Science at the National University of Singapore, where I will join Prof. Shao Lin's group. My research lies at the intersection of control, learning, and robotics, with a focus on humanoid manipulation, autonomous driving, and safety-critical systems.

I received dual undergraduate degrees from the UM-SJTU Joint Institute: B.Eng. in Mechanical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and B.S.E. in Computer Engineering from the University of Michigan. I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Zhongqiang Ren (SJTU), Prof. Tulga Ersal (UMich), and Prof. Changliu Liu (CMU).
