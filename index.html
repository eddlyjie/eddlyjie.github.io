Yijie Liao

Incoming PhD Student · Computer Science · National University of Singapore

I will be joining Professor Shao Lin's group at NUS. My research lies at the intersection of control, learning, and robotics, with a focus on humanoid manipulation, autonomous driving, and safety-critical systems.

I received dual undergraduate degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and Computer Engineering from the University of Michigan through the UM-SJTU Joint Institute. I have worked with Prof. Zhongqiang Ren (SJTU), Prof. Tulga Ersal (UMich), and Prof. Changliu Liu (CMU).
